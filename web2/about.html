The history

Equatorial Insurance Brokers Limited (EIB) is an independent short-term insurance broker offering a diverse range of insurance products and services.

Incorporated by the Registrar of companies of Malawi on the 9th of October 2017, Equatorial Insurance Brokers Ltd (EIB) was licenced by The Reserve Bank of Malawi as an Insurer Broker in August 2019 under the Insurance Act 2009 (as Amended) and the Financial Services Act 2010.

Equatorial operated as an insurance agency offering short-term insurances in the years of 2009 to 2019 representing the leading insurance company in Malawi, NICO General Insurance Company before being licenced as an Insurance Broker.

As an Insurance Agency, Equatorial became the Major Contributing Agent for NICO General Insurance Company in the years of 2016 and 2017 respectively due to its tremendous customer retention rate and outstanding customer relationship management.

MARKETING

Malawi has a young population, out of a population of about 18million, 70% are generally youth below the age of 30. Urban and rural populations, stand at 14% and 86% respectively. Sex ratio is at 94.2, which translates to 52% women.

OUTDOOR ADVERTISING

Outdoor advertising ranks third in Malawi in terms of reach, behind TV and radio. Even in the face of social media, outdoor advertising surprisingly still continues to dominate across product categories targeted at lower, middle and opulent communities.

TV JINGLES

According to MACRA 11% of households in Malawi own a working TV, 46% urban 6% rural areas. This means through TV jingles your company can reach up to 3 Million viewers, the majority of which are situated in urban areas.

RADIO JINGLES

MobTech Solutions has its own audio studio for recording jingles. Our voice actors and professional script writers have produced some of the best Radio Jingles in Malawi. Depending on your budget, we can avail you audio actors such as Gospel Kazako of ZBS, Falicia Ngoma, DJ Malimelo of Galaxy etc.

SOCIAL MEDIA & ONLINE ADVERTISING

Online advertising is a growing media outlet, its significance and relevance is increasing by the day. About 7% of households have access to internet in Malawi. As expected, 79% connect to the internet through mobile phone devices. It is estimated that about 2million people use the Internet in Malawi.

PHOTOGRAPHY & VIDEOGRAPHY

As already alluded to above MobTech Solutions has invested in one of the best video and photography studio in Malawi. The Photo and Videography department were established to assist cover corporate functions organized by our clients.

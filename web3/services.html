Malawi has a young population, out of a population of about 18million, 70% are generally youth below the age of 30. Urban and rural populations, stand at 14% and 86% respectively. Sex ratio is at 94.2, which translates to 52% women.

OUTDOOR ADVERTISING

Outdoor advertising ranks third in Malawi in terms of reach, behind TV and radio. Even in the face of social media, outdoor advertising surprisingly still continues to dominate across product categories targeted at lower, middle and opulent communities.

TV JINGLES

According to MACRA 11% of households in Malawi own a working TV, 46% urban 6% rural areas. This means through TV jingles your company can reach up to 3 Million viewers, the majority of which are situated in urban areas.

RADIO JINGLES

The Company

Freeline Limited is a freight and forwarding company that was registered under the Malawi Companies Act 2013. Our registration number is 10118010 and our Tax Pin Number is 20248028.


We provide innovative, effective, efficient and highly personalize Comprehensive Freight Services. We are also active in all aspect of Clearing, Forwarding, Air Cargo’ handling, Ship Agency and Warehousing Services.

Formular for success

Our formula for success in the customs and clearing services industry starts with these basic principles: We hire the right people, give them the best training and support them with strong leadership and opportunities to grow within the company.


We continually invest in technology that enhances our team's effectiveness and safety and work hard to develop team-oriented officers who are conscientious and motivated. We take pride in providing the most responsive customer service teams in the industry.


Our goal is to deliver exceptional service and security to our clients 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Our Service Offerings

Air freight cargo Handling.

Air freight is often the most suitable method of transport for urgent or time sensitive consignments. We understand that speed and reliability are of the essence.


Our Air Cargo Service has the capacity to handle all types of cargoes and isn’t limited to size. Irrespective of where you want to ship your item to, we have what it takes to get it there, fast.

clearing And forwarding

As part of our specialized services we carry out clearing and forwarding of Goods to or from any continent around the world. Our philosophy to be the best amongst the equals demands that we never compromise in our drive to ensure quality services. Our security and insurance policies are “second to none” in the industry


As licensed shipping agents, we have proven knowledge and experience in dealing with different government offices more particularly Malawian,Tanzanian, Mozambican, South African ports, immigration, and customs to facilitate the processing of the needed documents for vessels calling at Tanzanian, Mozambican, South African ports.

Shipping Agency Services.

We are competent to assist all kind of ships in any part of the world thanks to a wide and chooses network of partner agencies.

Our expertise in the shipping business allows us to provide port agency services all around the world with the highest standards.
